# 🎮 Lucky Bricks: Roguelike Guide

A strategic roguelike journey begins where your choices determine both survival and high scores.

---

## 1. ✨ Core System: Risk & Reward

Players directly design the game's difficulty and scoring efficiency by choosing **Modifiers**. It is up to you whether to take risks for high scores or choose safety for survival.

---

## 2. 💎 Lock Bricks & Modifiers

In every round, **5 Lock Bricks** are generated in random locations. Destroying a Lock Brick reveals a random effect, and you can select one of the acquired effects to accumulate after clearing the round.

### [Acquisition Probability & Details by Effect]

| No. | Effect Name | Detailed Function | Probability |
| :-- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | Reduce Bar Size | Decreases paddle length (+3 pts per brick) | 19.23% |
| 2 | Increase Bar Size | Increases paddle length (-1 pts per brick) | 19.23% |
| 3 | Increase Ball Speed | Increases ball movement speed (+3 pts per brick) | 19.23% |
| 4 | Decrease Ball Speed | Decreases ball movement speed (-1 pts per brick) | 19.23% |
| 5 | Score Bonus | Instantly adds +100 pts to current score | 19.23% |
| 6 | Score x 2 | Applies a x2 multiplier to current total score | 3.85% |

*   **Re-roll**: Available once per round. Watch an ad to replace one effect in the list based on the set probabilities.
*   **Ad-Free Benefit**: Players who purchased 'Remove Ads' can re-roll instantly without watching an advertisement.

---

## 3. ❤️ Life & Difficulty Management

As stages progress, the ball's base speed gradually increases, making systematic life management essential.

*   **Starting Lives**: Start with a total of **3** (1 Base + 2 Bonus).
*   **Life Acquisition**:
    *   Gain **+1 Life** for every **5-hit combo** achieved during gameplay.
    *   Acquire extra lives by watching an ad after Bonus or Boss rounds (Instantly granted for 'Remove Ads' users).
*   **Max Capacity**: You can hold up to a maximum of **5 lives**. The game ends when lives reach 0.

---

## 4. 👾 Boss Round Environment Gimmicks

In Boss rounds appearing every 5 stages, special environmental gimmicks are activated to hinder your play.

| Theme | Gimmick Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| 🌲 **Wood** | Decreases Bar (paddle) movement speed |
| ⏳ **Desert** | Increases randomness of ball bounce angles |
| 🌊 **Ocean** | Ball speed changes dynamically in real-time |
| ⛏️ **Mine** | Increased brick durability (Requires 2 hits to destroy) |
| ❄️ **Ice** | Reduced Bar friction causing sliding effects |
| 🔮 **Magic** | Ball irregularly disappears and reappears |
| 🌋 **Volcano** | Bar size continuously shrinks over time |
| 🌀 **Dimension** | Ball is re-launched in a random direction upon entering a rift |
| 🔥 **Hell** | **All previous boss gimmicks stacked** + Inverted Bar controls |
