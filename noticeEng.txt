[Known Issues]
  We have identified the following issues that may cause inconvenience during gameplay. We are currently working on a fix as a top priority.

  - Offline Mode Entry Error: Occasional issues with game execution when a network connection is unavailable.
  - Record Synchronization Issue: Rare instances where season records may appear incorrectly or reset unexpectedly in certain environments.

[Upcoming Update Preview]
  The following improvements and bug fixes will be implemented in the next update:

  - Performance Optimization: FPS Limit Unlock (Supporting smoother gameplay on PC and high-end devices).
