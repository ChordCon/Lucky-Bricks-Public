We have identified an issue with the World Ranking system and would like to provide an update.

  We have confirmed an issue where scores are not uploaded to the World Ranking (Server) unless they exceed the user's existing Personal Best Score.

- Status Update
  We have identified the cause and are working on a fix to be released as soon as possible.
  Until the fix is applied, please note that rankings will only update when you break your personal high score.
